About Geifon

A small company with a narrow definition of done.

Geifon LLC is an independent software studio. We build a handful of focused products — each one started because a specific person had a specific problem that the existing software simply ignored.

Why we exist

Most software grows by addition. Another tab, another setting, another upsell — until the thing that made it useful in the first place is buried three menus deep. We started Geifon to work the other way around: take one real, underserved problem, solve it completely, and then stop.

We are deliberately small, and we intend to stay that way. Being small lets us care about the details that never show up on a feature comparison — how fast the app opens, whether it works with no signal, whether you can understand it without a tutorial. We are not trying to win a chart. We are trying to build the tool you quietly keep using after the novelty wears off.

Everything we ship carries the same name because it carries the same standard. If it has Geifon on it, it earned its place.

How we work

01

Start with a real problem

Every product begins with someone who is genuinely stuck — not a market we'd like to enter. If we can't name the person and the moment they're frustrated, we don't build it.

02

Ship focused, not finished

We would rather do one thing completely than ten things partially. Scope is a feature, and saying no is most of the work. The result does less, on purpose, and does it well.

03

Earn the install

Useful on day one, worth paying for by month two. No dark patterns, no rented attention — a product has to justify its place on your phone or desktop on its own merits.

04

Build to last

Offline-first where it matters, durable data you own, and decisions we can still stand behind in five years. We build software the way we'd want the software we depend on to be built.
